# 高中英语试讲逐字稿

> **课题**：Reducing Water Pollution in the Li River  
> **教材**：人教版必修二 Unit 3 Environmental Protection (Using Language)  
> **时长**：8分钟  
> **授课类型**：阅读课（报告类文本）  
> **课型**：Reading for Writing

---

## 一、Pre-reading 导入环节（约1.5分钟）

### 1. Greeting & Lead-in（约1分钟）

Good morning, everyone! It's wonderful to see you all today.

*(走到讲台中央，展示漓江图片/手指课本图片)*

Now, look at this beautiful picture. **Do you know where this is?**

*(稍作停顿，期待回答)*

Yes, exactly! This is the **Li River** — 漓江 — one of the most famous rivers in China, located in Guilin, Guangxi.

*(用赞赏的语气)*

The Li River is famous for its stunning scenery — the green mountains, the clear water, and the peaceful atmosphere. It attracts **millions of tourists** every year.

*(语气转折，略带忧虑)*

But here's a question for you: **When millions of tourists visit a place every year, what problems might this cause?**

*(点头，做出引导的手势)*

Think about it... garbage? Pollution? Damage to nature?

*(肯定学生)*

Excellent thinking! And that's exactly what today's report is about.

---

### 2. Introducing the Text Type（约0.5分钟）

Today, we're going to read a **report** — a special type of text that:
- Presents a **problem**
- Analyzes the **causes**
- Describes the **solutions**

*(板书：Report = Problem → Causes → Solutions)*

The title of our report is **"Reducing Water Pollution in the Li River"**.

*(板书课题)*

Now, let's read and find out: **What happened to the Li River, and what has been done to save it?**

---

## 二、While-reading 精读环节（约5分钟）

### Task 1: Skimming for Main Ideas（约1分钟）

First, let's do some quick reading.

I'll give you **one minute** to skim through the four paragraphs and match each paragraph with its main function.

*(手指课本/PPT上的表格)*

Look at the table in Activity 3:

| Paragraph 1 | Paragraph 2 | Paragraph 3 | Paragraph 4 |
|:-----------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| Introducing the topic | _______ | _______ | _______ |

What is each paragraph mainly about? 

*(看表计时)*

Ready? Go!

*(等待约45秒)*

OK, time's up! Let's check together.

*(手指向文本，逐一确认)*

- **Paragraph 1**: Introducing the topic — 引出话题
- **Paragraph 2**: Describing the **problems** — 描述问题
- **Paragraph 3**: Presenting some **measures/solutions** — 介绍措施
- **Paragraph 4**: Presenting **further measures** — 进一步措施

*(板书：P1-Topic → P2-Problems → P3-Solutions → P4-Further Solutions)*

Excellent! You've grasped the structure of this report. This is a typical **problem-solution** structure, which is very common in environmental reports.

---

### Task 2: Scanning for Details — The Problems（约1.5分钟）

Now let's look more closely at **Paragraph 2**.

*(朗读提问)*

**What were some of the negative effects on the Li River as a result of the growth in tourism?**

Let's find the details together.

*(手指课文关键词，边读边板书)*

According to the text, the problems came from several sources:

| Source | Problem |
|:------:|:--------|
| 🚶 **Tourists** | threw **garbage** into the river |
| 🚢 **Tour boats** | kitchens used lots of **oil**, often thrown into water |
| 🏭 **Enterprises** | commercial and industrial waste ended up in the river |
| 🌾 **Farmers** | used **chemicals** to increase crop production |

*(用手势强调因果关系)*

And what was the result? The text tells us:

> "These chemicals led to severe water quality issues, causing a **decrease** in the number of fish species."

*(板书：chemicals → water quality issues → fewer fish)*

So you see, the pollution formed a **chain reaction** — 连锁反应. Tourism growth led to population growth, which led to more waste, more industry, and more chemicals. Everything is connected!

---

### Task 3: Scanning for Details — The Solutions（约1.5分钟）

Now, here comes the good news!

**What measures have been taken to improve the Li River's water quality?**

Let's look at **Paragraphs 3 and 4**.

*(边指课文，边板书分类)*

The government took a **comprehensive initiative** — 综合性举措. Let me organize these measures into categories:

| Category | Measures |
|:--------:|:---------|
| 🏗️ **Infrastructure** | Built waste water treatment facilities (污水处理设施) |
| 🗑️ **Waste Management** | Improved collection and transport of household waste |
| 🏭 **Industry Control** | Closed or moved polluting enterprises; set strict regulations |
| 🚢 **Tourism Regulation** | New rules for tour boat routes and garbage disposal |
| 📺 **Public Awareness** | Used media to spread environmental awareness |
| 👮 **Enforcement** | Regular **inspections** and **fines** for abuses |

*(强调两个重要政策)*

And in Paragraph 4, two important national initiatives are mentioned:

*(板书高亮)*

1. **"Water Ten Plan"** — 水十条
2. **"River Chief System"** — 河长制

*(解释河长制)*

The "River Chief System" is very interesting — it holds **senior officials personally responsible** for reducing water pollution. This means if the river is polluted, the local leader is accountable!

*(用积极的语气)*

With all these measures, the text concludes:

> "China's waterways are heading towards a **clean and sustainable future**."

That's very encouraging, isn't it?

---

### Language Focus: Key Vocabulary（约1分钟）

Before we move on, let's quickly look at some important words from this report:

*(指向课文中的加粗词汇)*

| Word | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|:-----|:----:|:-----|:-----|
| **volume** | n. | 大量、数量 | an increasing volume of tourists |
| **garbage** | n. | 垃圾 | threw garbage into the river |
| **enterprise** | n. | 企业 | commercial and industrial enterprises |
| **restore** | v. | 恢复 | restore the river's original beauty |
| **conservation** | n. | 保护 | water conservation |
| **campaign** | n. | 运动、活动 | environmental campaigns |
| **inspection** | n. | 检查 | carry out inspections regularly |
| **fine** | v./n. | 罚款 | fine tourist organisations for abuses |

*(用手势强调)*

These are very useful words for writing about environmental issues. Remember them!

## 三、Post-reading 读后环节（约1分钟）

### Discussion & Summary

Now, let's think deeper.

*(提出思考问题)*

This report shows us a successful example of environmental protection. But I want you to think about:

**Is there any river, lake, or natural area near YOUR home that faces similar problems?**

*(停顿让学生思考)*

Maybe the river in your hometown... or a park nearby... or even the ocean?

*(引导思考)*

After class, your task is to:
1. **Choose** an environmental problem in your local community
2. **Research** what's causing it and what actions have been taken
3. **Write** a similar report following this structure:

*(手指板书结构)*

```
Paragraph 1: Introduce the problem
Paragraph 2: Describe the causes
Paragraph 3: Present the solutions
Paragraph 4: Further measures or outlook
```

## 四、Summary 总结环节（约0.5分钟）

*(手指板书，快速总结)*

Let's wrap up today's lesson.

We learned about:

### 📌 Key Takeaways

1. The **structure** of an environmental report: Problem → Causes → Solutions
2. The **pollution problems** of the Li River caused by tourism and development
3. The **comprehensive measures** taken by the government
4. Important policies like the **"Water Ten Plan"** and **"River Chief System"**

*(真诚地看向学生)*

Remember, protecting our environment is **everyone's responsibility**. As the report says:

And YOU can be part of that future!

*(微笑收尾)*

## 🎯 教学目标

### 语言能力目标
- 能够识别并理解报告类文本的结构特点
- 能够获取并梳理漓江水污染问题及治理措施的关键信息
- 能够掌握 volume, garbage, enterprise, restore, conservation 等核心词汇

### 思维品质目标
- 能够分析问题与措施之间的逻辑关系
- 能够运用分类、归纳等方法整理文本信息

### 文化意识目标
- 了解中国环保政策（水十条、河长制）
- 增强环境保护意识和责任感

### 学习能力目标
- 能够运用 skimming 和 scanning 策略进行阅读
- 能够借鉴范文结构进行环保报告写作

## ✅ 试讲注意事项

### 1. 声音与节奏
- 声音洪亮，语速适中（每分钟120-150词）
- 关键词适当放慢、重读（如 "garbage", "enterprises", "Water Ten Plan"）
- 问题后留出2-3秒思考时间

### 2. 肢体语言
- 保持微笑，眼神覆盖全场
- 用手势引导学生注意力（指向板书、课本）
- 适当走动，拉近与学生的距离

### 3. 板书技巧
- 边讲边写，层次分明
- 用不同颜色区分问题（红色）和措施（绿色）
- 结构图要清晰、对称

### 4. 时间控制
- While-reading 是核心，确保充分展开
- 准备好缩减版本以应对时间紧张
- 结尾干净利落，不超时

### 5. 互动设计
- 问题设计有层次：Yes/No → Wh-questions → How/Why
- 给予积极反馈：Good! / Exactly! / Well done! / That's a great point!
- 若无人回答，可改为选择题或自问自答

---

## 🎭 面试加分项

1. **联系实际**：提到学生家乡的环保问题，体现"学以致用"
2. **文化自信**：介绍"河长制"等中国特色政策，展现家国情怀
3. **学科融合**：涉及地理（漓江位置）、生态（食物链）等跨学科知识
4. **情感升华**：结尾强调"环保是每个人的责任"，体现德育渗透
5. **读写结合**：明确后续写作任务，体现"以读促写"的教学理念
